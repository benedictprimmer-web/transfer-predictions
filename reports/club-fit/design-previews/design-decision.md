# Club-Fit Design Decision

Design A remains the selected editorial direction because the review is citation-heavy and caveat-sensitive. The final visual MVP uses an 8-page deterministic ReportLab implementation: cover/decision frame, two Arsenal candidate pages, two Chelsea candidate pages, a cross-candidate evidence/workflow page, and two methodology/reference pages.

The fixed ten-player set and accepted evidence classifications are preserved. The redesign changes presentation only: structured card headers, six badge dimensions per player, restrained Arsenal/Chelsea accents, non-ranking comparison table, workflow panel, readable references and rendered-page QA artifacts.

The committed HTML/CSS files are design prototypes. The final PDF is generated by the deterministic ReportLab renderer in `scripts/generate_club_fit_review.py`; it is not browser-printed from the HTML prototypes. Rendered PNG pages and the contact sheet are generated with `python3 scripts/render_club_fit_pdf.py` under `reports/club-fit/rendered-pages/` for human visual inspection.
